ABOUT

National institute of technology,Warangal offers a very nutrutios and healthy diets to it's students from all over India.There are four private messes spread across the campus.The messes are alloted to students based on their year of study to avoid crowds.Students must also pay the fee on daily basis.
For any quiries and complaints ,email messincharge@nitw.ac.in
